About

Practical wine calculators with clear assumptions

BottleMath is a lightweight reference site for buyers, collectors, hospitality teams, event planners, and wine enthusiasts who need quick answers without opening a spreadsheet.

What this site does

The site publishes browser-based calculators for common wine decisions: landed cost, tax and duty estimates, event planning, restaurant pricing, cellar economics, serving guidance, and winemaking math.

Each tool is designed to be fast, transparent, and useful on mobile as well as desktop.

How the calculators are built

Calculators use simple formulas implemented in client-side JavaScript so your inputs stay in your browser unless you choose to enable optional analytics.

Rates, assumptions, and examples are based on publicly available guidance where possible, then simplified for planning use. The tools are intended to help with estimation, not to replace merchant quotes, customs rulings, or professional advice.

Editorial standards

Pages are written to answer a specific wine question clearly and without filler. Where rates or rules can change, the copy should state the scope and encourage users to verify the latest official figures before paying money.

Commercial content and informational content should stay separate. Sponsored placements, affiliate links, and display ads should be labeled so readers can tell the difference between guidance and monetization.

Monetization and independence

The site is intended to generate revenue through advertising first and may later include affiliate links or commercial partnerships. That does not change the goal of the calculators: they should remain useful even if a reader never clicks an ad or partner link.

More detail is available on the Affiliate Disclosure page.
