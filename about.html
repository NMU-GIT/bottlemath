About

Practical wine calculators with clear assumptions

BottleMath is a lightweight reference site for wine buyers, collectors, event planners, hospitality teams, and enthusiasts who need quick numbers without opening a spreadsheet.

What BottleMath does

BottleMath publishes browser-based calculators for practical wine decisions: landed cost, tax and duty estimates, wedding quantities, restaurant pricing, cellar economics, and serving guidance. The tools are designed to be fast, transparent, and useful on mobile as well as desktop.

Calculators run in the browser, use editable assumptions where rates can change, and are intended for planning rather than professional advice. For site questions, corrections, or privacy requests, use the Contact page.
