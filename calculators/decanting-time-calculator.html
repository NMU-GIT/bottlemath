FAQ

Do very old wines need long decanting?

Often no. Older wines may need brief or slow decanting primarily for sediment separation.

Should sparkling wine be decanted?

Generally no. Sparkling wines are usually served directly after chilling.

Is this a strict rule?

Use it as a baseline and adjust with taste checks during service.
