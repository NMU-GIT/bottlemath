How It Works

This calculator estimates total carrying cost of holding a cellar over time.

  1. Enter bottle count and storage horizon in years.
  2. Input monthly storage charge, insurance rate, and annual handling/admin costs.
  3. Review total carrying cost and per-bottle-per-year expense.

FAQ

Why include insurance separately?

Insurance scales with collection value and can be a meaningful share of carrying costs.

Can this model mixed-value cellars?

Yes. Use weighted average bottle value or run separate scenarios for tiers.

Is home cellar modeled here?

Yes, by adjusting monthly storage and handling assumptions to your setup.
