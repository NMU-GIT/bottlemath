How It Works

This calculator estimates sugar mass needed to increase must Brix to a target level.

  1. Enter current Brix, desired target Brix, and total must volume.
  2. The tool computes Brix delta and estimated sugar requirement.
  3. Review expected sugar mass and approximate potential ABV increase.

FAQ

Is chaptalization legal everywhere?

No. Legal allowances vary by region, appellation, and vintage conditions.

Why is ABV gain approximate?

Fermentation efficiency and yeast performance can shift realized alcohol yield.

Should sugar be added all at once?

Many winemakers stage additions and re-measure to reduce overshoot risk.
