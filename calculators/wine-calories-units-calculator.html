How It Works

This calculator estimates energy and alcohol units from ABV and serving size.

  1. Enter serving size, ABV, number of servings, and bottle size.
  2. The tool converts alcohol content into UK units per serving and bottle.
  3. It then estimates calories and totals for your selected number of servings.

FAQ

How are UK units calculated?

UK units are calculated as milliliters multiplied by ABV percent divided by 1000.

Why are calories approximate?

Residual sugar and style differences affect energy beyond alcohol-only calculations.

Can I compare small and large pours?

Yes. Change serving size to compare unit and calorie impact directly.
