How It Works

This calculator converts an in-bond purchase into an estimated all-in landed cost.

  1. Enter case details: case count, bottles per case, bottle size, and ABV.
  2. Enter in-bond case price and non-tax costs such as shipping, storage, and fees.
  3. Review estimated duty, VAT, landed total, and per-bottle cost.

FAQ

Is duty linked to ABV?

Yes. The tool estimates duty from litres of pure alcohol (LPA), which depends on bottle volume and ABV.

Does this include every merchant fee?

Only the fees you enter. Add any broker, transfer, or delivery costs in the fee inputs for a more realistic estimate.

Can I use this for final purchase decisions?

Use it for planning, then confirm current rates and supplier charges before payment.
