How It Works

This calculator estimates realized resale return from a wine investment position.

  1. Enter original purchase total, holding period, and annual carrying costs.
  2. Add expected gross sale value plus commission, fixed fees, and tax assumptions.
  3. Review net cash in, net profit or loss, break-even sale value, and annualized return.

FAQ

Why can annualized return show N/A?

If cash-flow assumptions do not produce a positive realizable outcome, annualization may be undefined.

Does tax apply to losses?

In this model, tax applies only when gain before tax is positive.

Can I compare auction vs broker routes?

Yes. Run separate scenarios with different commission and fee assumptions.
