How It Works

This tool checks your carried wine volume against an allowance and estimates excess charges.

  1. Select a destination preset or enter a custom allowance in liters.
  2. Enter bottles carried, bottle size, and estimated bottle value.
  3. Review excess liters, excess bottles, and estimated total charges.

FAQ

Do all travelers get the same allowance?

Not always. Residency status, route, and trip origin can change eligibility and limits.

Does this handle connecting flights?

It is a planning estimate. Multi-leg journeys can apply different rules by final entry point.

What if I stay within allowance?

The calculator will show zero excess and zero estimated charges.
