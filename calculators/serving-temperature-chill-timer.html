How It Works

FAQ

Is freezer timing exact?

No. Freezer performance varies, so use the estimate as a guide and check temperature periodically.

Why does bottle size matter?

Larger formats change temperature more slowly than smaller bottles.

Can I use this for warming reds?

Yes. Select counter warming and provide ambient room temperature.
