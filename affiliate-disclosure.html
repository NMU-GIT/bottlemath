Affiliate and Advertising Disclosure

Last updated: March 23, 2026

How monetization may work

This site is built to earn revenue primarily from display advertising. It may also include affiliate links to wine-related products or services such as storage providers, event suppliers, books, glassware, or software tools.

What an affiliate link means

If you click an affiliate link and later make a purchase, the site may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Not every outgoing link is an affiliate link, and the presence of a link does not imply a formal partnership unless clearly stated.

Editorial independence

Calculators and supporting content should be published because they solve a user problem, not because a product pays to appear near them. If sponsored content or paid placements are added later, they should be labeled clearly.

Ads versus recommendations

Display ads are usually served automatically by an ad network and are distinct from editorial recommendations. A product appearing in an ad should not be interpreted as an endorsement by the site.

Questions

If you want to ask about a commercial relationship or a disclosure concern, use the details on the Contact page.
