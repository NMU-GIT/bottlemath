What you'll find here

Each guide explains a specific wine buying or planning question in plain terms, then points you to the calculator that helps you turn the answer into a decision.

The focus is practical: cost comparison, tax and duty, restaurant pricing, wedding planning, and the mistakes that make wine decisions look cheaper or simpler than they really are.

How the guides are used

Some readers arrive because they want a direct answer first and a calculator second. Others already know the question and need help translating it into an editable model. The guides are written to bridge that gap: enough context to make the numbers intelligible, without turning the site into a generic wine magazine.
